 ![Me](/assets/img/home.jpg)

I am a Maltese structural engineering student living in Malta. I currently pursue an M.Eng in Structural Engineering at the University of Malta.

I have a particular interest in parametric architecture and the integration of organic forms. In the past 5 years I have worked along side reputable architects and engineers which has given me experience and know-how in vasts aspects of civil engineering.

Recently, I have worked with FabLab Valletta to design and build a representation model of Malta International Airport. As an entrepreneur I have co-founded Bearn Limited, creators of CloudBosses and the grocery shopping platform TillFree.
